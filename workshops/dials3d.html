DIALS-3: 22nd May 2013 (Cambridge, UK)¶

DIALS update meeting with our collaborators at the MRC Laboratory of Molecular Biology in Cambridge. The day started with presentations from the Diamond and CCP4 developers ended with a presentation about reflection formats and containers for future handling of unmerged reflection data incorporating multiple lattice diffraction data management.
